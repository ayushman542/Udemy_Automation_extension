▶️ Follow This Step To use 👇

  • 1️⃣: Firstly, turn on the extension and log in to Udemy
  • 2️⃣: Open any course
  • 3️⃣: The course is open, press Alt+G
  • 4️⃣: Then, a popup will appear; click OK
  • 5️⃣: After that, press Alt+C
  • 6️⃣: Another popup will appear; click OK
  • 7️⃣: Repeat the sequence: First Alt+G, then Alt+C, then Alt+G again, and finally Alt+C
  • 8️⃣: The course will automatically be completed
